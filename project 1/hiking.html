Ice climbing & Hiking On Glaciers



Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year round. The your offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Solheimajokull glacier. Add even more adventure tou your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.

Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanos and during the summer time, a Glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name of Iceland!
